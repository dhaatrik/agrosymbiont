# Product Guidelines

## Core Principles
1. **Accessibility First:** Ensure the platform is usable by everyone, particularly in rural environments. Interfaces should be responsive, clean, and intuitive.
2. **Performance Oriented:** With offline support via PWA, prioritize lightweight assets and fast loading times to accommodate users with low bandwidth.
3. **Data-Driven Trust:** Ensure all eco-friendly solutions, calculators, and educational content are fact-based and clearly presented.

## Design System & UX
- **Theme Support:** Adapt to user's system preferences (Dark/Light mode).
- **Micro-interactions:** Use subtle animations to guide users without overwhelming them.
- **Glassmorphism UI:** Implement a clean, modern aesthetic with 3D background elements where appropriate, but fallback gracefully on low-end devices.

## Content Strategy
- **Tone:** Professional, encouraging, and educational. Avoid overly complex jargon without clear definitions.
- **Localization:** Support multi-language capabilities (i18n) to serve a diverse farming community.
